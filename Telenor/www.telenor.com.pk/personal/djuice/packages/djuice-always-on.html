How do I convert to the Always On plan?

Dial *345*44# to convert to this plan. Migration is free of cost for both Telenor & djuice users for limited time.

What are the data charges on this plan?

The data pulse is 10KB. Charges per pulse are Rs. 0.12 (inclusive of tax). On spending Rs.3 (including tax), you get free 50 MB of basic internet at 50 Kbps

What are the call/SMS charges on this price-plan?

Telenor and other network call charges are Rs. 1.5/30sec. SMS charges are Rs. 0.6 / SMS (both tax inclusive)

What is the download speed I’ll get when using data without bundles?

The default speed of the price plan is throttled at 50KBPS. To use internet at regular speed, please subscribe to your favorite add-ons.

What happens when I buy an Internet bundle or Social Pack?

You will get regular 3G speeds on usage of all 3G Internet bundles and Social Pack.

 What is the benefit of using the djuice Always On plan?

This plan gives you freedom from high regular Internet tariffs. If you exceed your 3G bundle limit or your phone uses background data when you don’t have an Internet bundle, you will not be charged more than Rs.3 for the day.
