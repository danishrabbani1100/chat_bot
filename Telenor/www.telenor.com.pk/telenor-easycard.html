Offer Eligibility:

All prepaid customers (Telenor and djuice) are eligible for this offer.

 

How to Subscribe:

  • Easyload/Retailer Menu: Ask your nearest retailer for “EasyCard” recharge. The retailer will directly subscribe this offer for you.
  • Scratch Card (350 only): Ask your nearest retailer for the “EasyCard” and load this card to subscribe to the offer
  • Like any regular scratch card, you can load this Card by dialing *555*<14 digit code on card>#.

 

Validity:

  • EasyCard and EasyCard Plus are valid for 30 days.
  • EasyCard Weekly is valid for 7 days.

 

Terms and Conditions: 

  • Each re-subscription will enhance the validity by up to the duration of the selected offer, from the time the offer is re-subscribed.
  • These offers can be re-subscribed, with each subsequent re-subscription adding to the previous resources.
  • Customers who take this offer shall have their existing recharge validity enhanced up to a maximum of 90 days.
  • Telenor reserves the right to withdraw the offer at any time.
  • Offer mechanics and price are subject to change at any time.
  • For details on regional taxation, please Click Here.

Are there any additional charges in the offer?

No, there are no additional charges in the offer.

How can I check the free resources?

The free resources can be checked by dialing *123# 

How do I avail this offer?

You can avail this offer by either of the following methods:

  • Asking a retailer specifically for the “easycard” based easyload of Rs. 300
  • Buying the specifically designated “easycard offer” scratch card from retailer and loading it via the normal card loading method (details also given at back of card)
