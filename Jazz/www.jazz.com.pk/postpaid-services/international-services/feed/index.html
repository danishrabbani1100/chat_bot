IDD USA Offer

Continuing the tradition of offering best value to customers, postpaid brings yet another unparalleled IDD offer for USA – the most affordable connectivity beyond borders. Now Postpaid customers can call USA at extremely low rates of Rs. 14.99+tax/ 30 minutes.

  • Customer will have to dial 456 before the number e.g. he will have to dial 456001city<code><number>
  • Every call that the customer makes to USA with the prefix 456 will be charged at Rs. 14.99 +tax for 30 minutes
  • The offer is available 24 Hours a day
  • No daily or one time subscription is required
  • This offer is not applicable to premium numbers of USA
  • Government taxes apply on Rs. 14.99 / 30 minutes and the deduction will be Rs. 17.91 / 30 minutes
  • Offer is available for all Postpaid customers
  • Limited Time Offer

IDD Canada Offer

Now for the first time in Pakistan, call Canada at the exciting new rates of Rs. 14.99+tax / 30 minutes. Just dial 456 before the international number.

  • Every call that the customer makes to Canada with the prefix will be charged at Rs. 14.99+tax for 30 minutes (half an hour)
  • Customer will have to dial 456 before the number e.g. 456-00-1- <citycode>- <phone number>
  • The offer is available 24 Hours a day
  • No daily or one time subscription is required
  • Government taxes apply on Rs. 14.99 / 30 minutes and the deduction will be Rs. 17.91 / 30 minutes
  • This offer is not available to premium numbers of Canada (which include Yukon, New Territories and Independents)
  • Offer is available for all Customer Postpaid customers
  • Limited Time Offer

IDD UK Offer

Expanding Postpaid IDD Offer destinations, postpaid brings more value for its customers by including UK Landline to the portfolio of 456 Prefix based offer destinations.

  • Every call that the customer makes to UK (Landline) with the prefix will be charged at Rs. 5+tax for 15 minutes

  • Offer is not applicable to premium numbers (any given destination) OR mobile numbers (UK)
