Roam Info

Jazz brings to you an SMS based service that will serve as an information log for anybody interested in roaming abroad!

Through this service, customers who intend to go abroad will be able to know call, SMS or data tariffs for all mobile operators in any country they plan to visit. This would help them in deciding which operator to opt for once they land in that country.

In order to avail this service, the customer will have to follow the procedure below:

For Postpaid Roaming Information: SMS Postpaid<space>Country name to 4849
For Prepaid Roaming Information: SMS Prepaid<space>Country name to 4849
For GPRS Roaming Information: SMS GPRS<space>Country name to 4849

  • Sample Procedure
  • FAQs

Sample Procedure

For Postpaid Roaming Information

SMS Text: Postpaid<space>Country name
Send to: 4849
Example: Postpaid France

For Prepaid Roaming Information

SMS text: Prepaid<space>Country name
Send to: 4849
Example: Prepaid France

For GPRS Roaming Information

SMS text: GPRS<space>Country name
Send to: 4849
Example: GPRS France

For further details call 111.

FAQs

What information does this service offer?

Through this service, you will be receiving an SMS containing roaming tariffs for local and International calls, SMS and GPRS associated with an international mobile operator in the country of your choice.

How much will I get charged to send an SMS?

Standard SMS charges of Rs. 2+tax apply.

Can this service be availed by any Jazz customer?

Yes, both prepaid and postpaid customers can avail this service.
