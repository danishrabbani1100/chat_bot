Upgrade/Downgrade Later Process:

  • Step 1: All bundle upgrade/downgrade requests submitted through m.jazz.com.pk portal will be saved on system for further processing.
  • Step 2: System will automatically check remaining volume and expiry of customer’s current bundle, when remaining data volume is less than 70 MB OR remaining bundle expiry is less than 24 hours then request for upgrade/downgrade will be initiated.
  • Step 3: After request initiation, if customer has enough balance for bundle upgrade/downgrade, then system will unsubscribe customer’s current bundle and upgrade/downgrade to customer’s desired bundle.
